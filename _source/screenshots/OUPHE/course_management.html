Course Management

You can access course packages available for certain OUP titles to use within the WebCT and Blackboard e-learning environments.

These content packages let you take advantage of the power of WebCT and Blackboard. You have the flexibility to arrange a variety of learning materials and build an online teaching resource customized for your course. Your students gain access to our available student resources, which will be offered on your institution's web site.

Oxford's partnership with WebCT and Blackboard helps you make teaching more efficient and learning more effective. Complete details are available from your sales representative.
